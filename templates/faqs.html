Frequently Asked Questions

What is SamvaadSetu?

SamvaadSetu is an AI-powered communication training platform that helps users improve their presentation and communication skills through real-time feedback and personalized coaching.

How does the AI feedback work?

Our AI analyzes your facial expressions, body language, speech patterns, and content delivery in real-time, providing instant feedback and suggestions for improvement.

Which languages are supported?

Currently, we support English, Hindi, and several other major languages. We're continuously adding more languages to our platform.

Is my practice data private?

Yes, all your practice sessions and personal data are completely private and secured with enterprise-grade encryption.

Can I use SamvaadSetu offline?

While some features require internet connectivity, we offer basic practice modules that can work offline.

How do I track my progress?

Our platform provides detailed analytics and progress tracking through your personal dashboard, showing improvements over time.
